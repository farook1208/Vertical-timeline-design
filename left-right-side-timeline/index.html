Here are the detailed checklists to get into a US University

The Complete Checklist for a US University

Standardized Test Scores (SAT/ACT):

Required by most universities. Start preparing early and aim for scores that meet the university's benchmarks.

Proof of English Proficiency:

Most of the universities accept IELTS and TOEFL scores as proof of proficiency in English language. This is a mandatory requirement for US education aspirants.

Transcripts:

Official transcripts from your previous schools, translated into English if necessary.

Letters of Recommendation:

Request strong academic and professional recommendation letters that highlight your skills and potential.

Statement of Purpose:

Craft a compelling essay outlining your academic goals, interests, and reasons for choosing the specific program and university.

Financial Aid Documents:

Proof of sufficient financial resources to cover your study expenses. This may include bank statements, scholarship letters, or sponsorships.

Application Fee:

Pay the application fee for each university you apply to.
